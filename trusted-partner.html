Tugboat is free for Digital Agencies

Today we’re announcing our Trusted Partner Program.

Don’t worry, there’s no fees for joining the program or silly badges to put on your website: really it’s just a form to fill out to make sure your clients have projects where they’ll get the most value from using Tugboat.

So why would an agency use Tugboat for their projects?

Put simply, you can collaborate with your team and engage stakeholders in ways never before possible. We've eliminated the technical barriers to sharing development work on projects. Tugboat builds a complete, working website for every pull request. We take new code, integrate it into the larger project, build your website and return a secure link to share with the team. This agile approach to QA keeps your progress visible while also receiving timely feedback from the client. It’s an awesome, collaborative way to work.

Here’s the deal.

Digital Agencies in our Trusted Partner Program use Tugboat for free on their own projects. Whether you want to use Tugboat on your company website or some other related project, you get Tugboat for free. Your clients also benefit by getting Tugboat for free for the first 3 months on their project they start with you. After the initial 3 months, clients receive our trusted partner rate for Tugboat (see below). When clients hire great agencies to do their work, it almost assuredly means these clients are going to also be great for Tugboat. And great clients have less overhead, hence the client partner rate.
